# Ad Operations Workflow – Accenture 3PAS

## Overview
This case study explains the typical ad trafficking and tracking workflow followed while working in the 3PAS (Third Party Ad Tracking Services) team at Accenture.

The role focused on ensuring accurate campaign setup, tracking implementation, and quality assurance before and after campaign launches.

---

## Role
Ad Trafficking Analyst

---

## Campaign Workflow

### 1. Campaign Brief Review
The process begins when campaign details are received from the client or internal campaign management team.

Information reviewed includes:
Campaign objectives
Ad formats
Creative assets
Campaign start and end dates
Publisher placements

---

### 2. Ad Tag Generation
Ad tags are generated through the ad server and prepared for publisher implementation.

Common tag components include:
Ad creative code
Click tracking URL
Impression tracking pixel
Redirect URLs

---

### 3. Tracking Implementation
Tracking pixels are implemented to measure campaign performance.

Typical tracking elements include:
Impression tracking
Click tracking
Conversion tracking
Video tracking when applicable

---

### 4. Creative QA Validation
Before campaign launch, creatives and tags are tested to ensure proper functionality.

QA checks include:
Creative dimensions
File size compliance
Click URL validation
Tracking pixel firing
Creative rendering across placements

---

### 5. Campaign Launch Support
Once QA is complete, tags are shared with publishers and campaigns are scheduled for launch according to the campaign timeline.

The team ensures:
On-time campaign activation
Correct placement implementation
Tracking verification

---

### 6. Post Launch Monitoring
After the campaign goes live, delivery metrics are monitored.

Metrics checked include:
Impressions
Clicks
Delivery pacing
Tracking accuracy

---

### 7. Discrepancy Investigation
If discrepancies appear between publisher and ad server data, investigation is performed.

Troubleshooting may involve:
Verifying tag implementation
Checking tracking pixels
Confirming reporting timelines
Coordinating with publisher teams

---

## Key Skills Used

Ad trafficking
Tracking pixel implementation
Campaign QA validation
Tag troubleshooting
Performance monitoring

---

## Tools Commonly Used in Ad Operations

Ad servers
Campaign tracking platforms
Reporting dashboards
Excel for campaign analysis

---

## Outcome

This workflow ensured campaigns were launched accurately, tracking was implemented correctly, and performance data remained reliable for campaign reporting and optimization.
